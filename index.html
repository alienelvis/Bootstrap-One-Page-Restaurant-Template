About Us

It all started one rainy and stormy night. We were sitting out on our patio, looking over the pond. A crack of lightning lit up the sky, and for a moment we saw a figure, out on the pond, in a rowboat, rowing methodically towards us. A chill went up our spines.

After another flash of lighting we could tell that the figure was only getting closer. I tried to yell at the others that we should run inside, but the thunder and rain was so loud that they did not hear me.

One more strike of lightning, and we could see that the figure had landed its boat on the shore near our patio. It was walking with stiff jerks and mechanical motions. Closer, and closer it came. Its eyes were glowing a bright orange. It carried something in its hands.

Suddenly it was upon us! A robot of antiquity, an eight foot tall monstrosity of rust and cogs! It was close enough for us to see what it held... a pizza!

Its large metal mouth opened, and a creaking, whirring voice came out, louder than we expected. It said, "I bring you the pizza of my kind, and demand we make more, so that I may return to my planet a hero!"

And that is (more or less) how Rowboat Robot Pizza was started nearly three years ago.
